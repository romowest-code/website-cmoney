My Story

20 years corporate. Wall Street. Tech. Project management. I made big machines run smooth—but I was disconnected from real work.

Then I picked up a toolbelt. Became a handyman in Denver. Got my contractor's license. Learned what it actually takes to run jobs, chase payments, and keep a business alive from the seat of a truck.

The software out there? Built for enterprises. $150/month. Fleet tracking for one van. Dashboards I'd never use.

So I built C-Money. Corporate brain meets contractor reality. Everything you need. Nothing you don't. $9.99/month.
