info Data Sources

Pricing data is compiled from industry surveys, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack, and BLS statistics. Actual rates vary significantly based on location, specialization, and market conditions. Use this as a starting point for your research.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Select your trade and service type
  2. Enter your zip code or city
  3. See average rates for your area
  4. Compare with high and low ranges

Pricing Strategy Tips

  • Don't compete on price alone—compete on value
  • Know your costs before setting prices
  • Raise rates annually (3-5% minimum)
  • Premium pricing attracts better customers
  • Include your expertise and reliability in your value proposition
